# 🛒 Super Store Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
The Super Store Dashboard is an interactive Power BI report designed to analyze sales performance, profitability, customer segmentation, regional contributions, and product category performance. The dashboard provides business stakeholders with a quick and comprehensive view of key metrics and actionable insights for decision-making.

---

## 🎯 Objective
To monitor and evaluate:
- Total Sales Performance
- Profitability Trends
- Product Demand
- Regional Revenue Contribution
- Customer Segment Distribution
- Monthly Revenue Trends
- Business Insights & Target Achievement

---

## 📈 Dashboard Components

### 1. Revenue by Region
Displays revenue generated across different regions:
- West Region – Highest Revenue Contributor
- East Region
- South Region
- Central Region

**Insight:** West region contributes approximately ₹91.89K, making it the top-performing region.

---

### 2. Revenue by Category
Compares revenue across product categories:
- Technology
- Furniture
- Office Supplies

**Insight:** Technology generates the highest revenue at approximately ₹652.85K.

---

### 3. Customer Segment Analysis
Visual representation of customer distribution:
- Consumer Segment – 63%
- Corporate Segment – 37%

**Insight:** Consumer customers form the majority of the customer base.

---

### 4. Monthly Revenue Trend
Line chart displaying revenue performance throughout the year.

**Key Findings:**
- Highest Revenue Month: November (₹290.39K)
- Lowest Revenue Month: February (₹48.40K)
- Strong growth observed during Q4.

---

### 5. Key Insights Panel
The dashboard highlights important business observations:

- West Region contributes the highest revenue.
- Technology category drives maximum sales.
- Consumer segment dominates customer distribution.
- November records the highest monthly revenue.
- Sales target achievement stands at 74% of the ₹2M target.
